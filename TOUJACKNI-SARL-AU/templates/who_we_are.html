TOUJACKNI STORE

TOUJACKNI Store is a company specialized in JACK sewing machines and all clothing equipment, founded in 2016 in Casablanca.
After +5 years of effort and hard work, TOUJACKNI Store opened a new branch in Hay Al Fadl, in addition to the original and first branch in Hay Al Baraka 2 of Casablanca, one of the best garment and textile industrial zones in Morocco.
Our headquarters are currently located in Hay Al Fadl, and TOUJACKNI Store is interested in providing a variety of new and used equipment that is widely consumed in the clothing and sewing market.
